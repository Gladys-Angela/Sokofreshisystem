Payment Methods

Secure and convenient ways to pay for your groceries

Available Payment Options

M-Pesa

Pay directly from your M-Pesa account. Fast, secure, and convenient mobile payment.

Credit/Debit Cards

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express cards for secure online payments.

Cash on Delivery

Pay with cash when your order is delivered to your doorstep. Available in select areas.

Bank Transfer

Make a direct bank transfer to our account. Order processing begins after payment confirmation.

How to Pay

M-Pesa Payment

1
Select M-Pesa at Checkout

Choose M-Pesa as your payment method when completing your order at checkout.

2
Enter Your Phone Number

Enter the M-Pesa registered phone number where you want to receive the payment request.

3
Confirm Payment Request

You'll receive an STK push notification on your phone. Enter your M-Pesa PIN to authorize the payment.

4
Payment Confirmation

Once the payment is successful, you'll receive a confirmation message and your order will be processed.

Credit/Debit Card Payment

1
Select Card Payment at Checkout

Choose Credit/Debit Card as your payment method when completing your order.

2
Enter Card Details

Enter your card number, expiration date, CVV, and cardholder name in our secure payment gateway.

3
Verify Payment

You may be redirected to your bank's 3D Secure verification page to complete the payment authentication.

4
Payment Confirmation

Once the payment is successful, you'll be redirected back to our website and receive a confirmation email.

Payment Security

SSL Encryption

All payment information is encrypted using 256-bit SSL technology to ensure your data remains private and secure.

PCI DSS Compliant

We adhere to Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards to protect your card information.

3D Secure

Additional security layer for card payments that verifies your identity with your card issuer.

At Soko freshi, we take your payment security seriously. We never store your complete credit card information on our servers. All payment processing is handled by trusted, industry-leading payment processors that maintain the highest security standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will my card be charged?

Your card will be charged immediately after you place your order. If for any reason we cannot fulfill your order, a full refund will be processed to your original payment method.

Is it safe to save my card details for future purchases?

Yes, it's safe to save your card details for future purchases. We use tokenization technology, which means your actual card details are not stored on our servers. Instead, a secure token is created and stored, which cannot be used to make purchases outside of our platform.

What happens if my M-Pesa payment fails?

If your M-Pesa payment fails, you'll receive a notification with the reason for the failure. You can try again or choose a different payment method. Common reasons for failure include insufficient funds, wrong PIN, or transaction limits.

Can I change my payment method after placing an order?

Once an order is placed, you cannot change the payment method through the website. However, you can contact our customer service team within 30 minutes of placing your order, and they may be able to assist you with changing the payment method if the order has not yet been processed.

Do you charge any additional fees for specific payment methods?

No, we don't charge any additional fees for any payment method. The price you see at checkout is the final price you'll pay, regardless of which payment method you choose.
