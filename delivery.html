Delivery Process

Order Confirmation
Once your order is placed, you'll receive an order confirmation email with your order details.
Order Processing
Our team carefully selects and packs your items to ensure freshness and quality.
Dispatch Notification
You'll receive an SMS when your order is dispatched with an estimated delivery time.
On the Way
Our delivery partner will contact you when they're approaching your location.
Delivery
Your order is delivered to your doorstep. You can inspect the items before accepting.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

Our standard delivery hours are from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, seven days a week. For express delivery, we deliver between 9:00 AM and 9:00 PM.

How can I track my delivery?

You can track your delivery through the "Orders" section in your account dashboard. We also send SMS updates with tracking information when your order is dispatched.

What if I'm not available during delivery?

If you're not available during the scheduled delivery time, our delivery partner will call you to arrange an alternative time. If we can't reach you, we'll leave a note and attempt delivery the next day.

Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Yes, you can change your delivery address before your order is dispatched. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible to request the change.

Do you deliver on weekends and public holidays?

Yes, we deliver seven days a week, including weekends and most public holidays. However, delivery times may be adjusted during major holidays.
