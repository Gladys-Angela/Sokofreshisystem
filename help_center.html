Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new product?

To add a new product, go to the "Add Product" page from the sidebar menu. Fill in all the required product details including name, category, price, and stock quantity. Select an image for your product and click "Add Product" to save it to your inventory.

How do I track my sales?

You can track your sales by visiting the "Sales" page in the sidebar menu. There you'll find detailed reports of all your sales, including daily, weekly, and monthly summaries. You can also filter sales by date range or product category.

When will I receive payment for my sales?

Payments are processed every Monday for the previous week's sales. The funds will be transferred to your registered bank account or mobile money account within 2-3 business days after processing.

How do I update my product inventory?

To update your product inventory, go to the "Products" page and click on the "Edit" button next to the product you want to update. You can modify the stock quantity and other product details from there.

How do I change my account information?

You can update your account information by clicking on your profile icon in the top right corner and selecting "Edit Profile". From there, you can update your personal information, contact details, and payment preferences.
