Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and operations.

How does Soko freshi ensure product quality?

At Soko freshi, quality is our priority. We work directly with certified farmers and trusted suppliers who follow strict quality standards. Our dedicated quality assurance team inspects all products before they enter our inventory, and we maintain optimal storage conditions to preserve freshness until delivery.

What are your delivery options and timeframes?

Soko freshi offers three flexible delivery options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Delivery: Next-day service available throughout Nairobi and surrounding areas (Ksh 150, free for orders above Ksh 2,000)
  • Express Delivery: Same-day delivery for orders placed before 10:00 AM (Ksh 300, free for orders above Ksh 5,000)
  • Scheduled Delivery: Select your preferred 2-hour delivery window up to 7 days in advance (Ksh 200, free for orders above Ksh 3,000)

All deliveries include real-time tracking through our mobile app and SMS notifications at key delivery milestones.

How does Soko freshi support local communities?

At Soko freshi, community support is integrated into our business model through several key initiatives:

  • Direct Sourcing Network: We've established partnerships with over 200 small-scale farmers across Kenya, eliminating middlemen and ensuring 80% of our produce comes from within 150km of our distribution centers.
  • Fair Compensation Program: Our farmers receive payments 15-20% above market rates, with guaranteed purchase agreements that provide income stability regardless of market fluctuations.
  • Soko freshi Foundation: We invest 3% of our annual profits in agricultural education, providing scholarships and training programs that have benefited over 500 young farmers since 2025.
  • Rural Infrastructure: Through our "Grow Together" initiative, we've helped develop cold storage facilities in 12 rural communities, reducing post-harvest losses by up to 40%.

These efforts create sustainable economic growth while ensuring you receive the freshest produce possible.

What is your quality guarantee policy?

Our "Farm-to-Table Freshness Guarantee" ensures your complete satisfaction:

  • 100% Satisfaction Promise: If any product fails to meet your expectations in terms of freshness, quality, or condition, we'll make it right immediately.
  • Simple Claims Process: Report quality concerns within 48 hours of delivery through our app, website, or customer service line (0758 599393). Include a photo if possible, but it's not required.
  • Flexible Resolution Options: Choose between a no-questions-asked refund to your original payment method (processed within 24 hours) or expedited replacement delivery at no additional cost.

This guarantee applies to all products, with no minimum purchase requirement and no return shipping necessary for perishable items.
