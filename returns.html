Returns & Refunds

Our hassle-free return policy and refund process

Our Return Policy

24-Hour Return Window

We accept returns within 24 hours of delivery for fresh produce and groceries to ensure quality concerns are addressed promptly.

No Questions Asked

If you're not satisfied with the quality of your products, we'll replace them or refund you with no questions asked.

Quick Refunds

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after the return is approved, directly to your original payment method.

At Soko freshi, we're committed to your satisfaction. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help. Our return policy allows you to return eligible products within 24 hours of delivery for fresh items and 7 days for non-perishable goods.

We understand that sometimes products may not meet your expectations. That's why we've made our return process simple and hassle-free. Please note that for hygiene and safety reasons, certain items cannot be returned once opened or used.

Return Process

1
Initiate Return Request

Log in to your account, go to "Orders" section, select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return, and click on "Return Items". Alternatively, you can call our customer service at +254 700 123 456.

2
Provide Return Reason

Select a reason for your return from the dropdown menu and provide any additional details that might help us improve our service. You can also upload photos of damaged or spoiled products.

3
Schedule Pickup

Choose a convenient date and time for our delivery partner to pick up the return items. For perishable goods, we may not require physical return but only photo evidence.

4
Return Verification

Once we receive your returned items, our quality team will verify the return reason. This process typically takes 1-2 business days.

5
Refund Processing

After verification, we'll process your refund to the original payment method. Refunds typically appear in your account within 3-5 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have to return an item?

For fresh produce, dairy, meat, and other perishable items, you have 24 hours from the time of delivery to initiate a return. For non-perishable items, you have 7 days from the delivery date.

Do I need to return the physical items for perishable goods?

For perishable items like fruits, vegetables, and dairy products, we typically don't require you to return the physical items. Instead, we may ask for photos as evidence of quality issues. This helps us improve our quality control process.

How will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed to the original payment method used for the purchase. If you paid by credit/debit card, the refund will be credited back to the same card. For M-Pesa payments, the refund will be sent to the same phone number. Cash on delivery payments will be refunded via M-Pesa or store credit.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, you can request an exchange for items of equal or lesser value. If you choose an item of higher value, you'll need to pay the difference. To request an exchange, follow the same return process but select "Exchange" instead of "Return" when initiating the request.

What if my order is missing items?

If your order is missing items, please contact our customer service within 24 hours of delivery. We'll verify the missing items against your order and either arrange for delivery of the missing items or process a refund for them.

Need help with a return?

Our customer service team is ready to assist you with any questions or concerns.
